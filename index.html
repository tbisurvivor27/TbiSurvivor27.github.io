My Story

I Survived.
Now I'm Living.

A traumatic brain injury changes everything — the way you think, the way you move, the way the world sees you. For too long, I let it define what I couldn't do.

That changes now. I'm casting off the lines aboard TBI Survivor, my 34ft trawler, and navigating America's Great Loop — roughly 6,000 miles of waterways connecting the eastern United States, the Gulf of Mexico, and the Great Lakes.

This isn't a vacation. It's a statement. To every TBI survivor who has been told to lower their expectations — this journey is for you.
